NextDonor (Personal Prototype)

A smarter way for campaigns and fundraisers to surface high-potential donors from public data.

NextDonor is a lightweight prototype I built to explore cleaner, faster ways to surface insights from public fundraising data. Most tools I’ve used felt clunky or noisy — so I created something that strips away complexity and puts relevance first.

Status: Personal prototype (non-commercial)

Role: Designer / Developer

Stack: React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
