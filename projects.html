NextDonor (Personal Prototype)

Exploring more intuitive ways to extract donor insights from public campaign finance data.

NextDonor is a self-initiated experiment to streamline political donor targeting workflows. Most tools I’ve encountered as a fundraiser are either too rigid or too noisy—so I designed a lightweight prototype to surface cleaner, faster insights from public databases.

Status: Personal prototype (non-commercial)

Role: Designer / Developer (built for learning)

Stack: React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL

Curious? Always open to feedback or collab—just reach out.
