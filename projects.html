California Women’s List

California Women’s List

As Finance Director, I lead statewide fundraising efforts to elect women running for office across California. Since stepping into the role, I’ve helped increase contributions by more than 62% while reducing operating costs so that more resources go directly to candidates.

I work closely with donors, sponsors, and organizational partners to build long-term support for women seeking office at both the state and local level. You can reach me at katerina@cawomenslist.org.

PoliticalDonorIQ

Non-Commercial Personal Project

PoliticalDonorIQ is a personal project exploring ways to make federal campaign finance data more accessible and easier to understand. Using public FEC data and AI-assisted tools, the platform generates structured insights designed to support research and analysis.

Status: Invite-only beta
Role: Designer / Developer / Product Architect
Stack: React, Deno, FEC API
Site: politicaldonoriq.com
Contact: info@politicaldonoriq.com
