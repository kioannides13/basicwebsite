Projects

A sample of projects and prototypes I’m currently working on outside of my full-time role.

California Women’s List

As Finance Director for California Women’s List, I help lead statewide efforts to support pro-choice women running for office in California. My focus is on scaling sustainable fundraising infrastructure, expanding institutional partnerships, and shaping our 10-year strategic growth vision. I work closely with donors, sponsors, and stakeholders to power early support for candidates positioned to lead.

NextDonor (Personal Prototype)

I built NextDonor as a self-initiated experiment to learn more about streamlining political donor targeting workflows from public databases. Most tools I’ve encountered as a fundraiser either lack flexibility or are burdened with unnecessary complexity, so I designed a lean prototype to explore how cleaner, more actionable data could be surfaced quickly from public databases.

Status: Personal prototype (non-commercial)
Role: Designer / Developer (built for learning)
Stack: React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
