California Women’s List

As Finance Director for California Women’s List, I lead statewide fundraising efforts to support women running for office across California. Since stepping into the role, I’ve helped increase contributions by more than 62% while reducing operating costs so that more resources go directly to candidates.

I work closely with donors, sponsors, and organizational partners to build long-term support for women seeking office at both the state and local level.

Contact: katerina@cawomenslist.org

PoliticalDonorIQ

Non-commercial personal project

PoliticalDonorIQ is a personal project exploring ways to make federal campaign finance data more accessible and easier to understand. Using public FEC data and AI-assisted tools, the platform generates structured insights designed to support research and analysis.

Currently in invite-only beta. Built with React, Deno, and public FEC data.

Site: politicaldonoriq.com
Contact: info@politicaldonoriq.com
